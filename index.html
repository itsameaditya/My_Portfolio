Featured Project

FitLake — Fitness Analytics Data Lakehouse

A production-grade data lakehouse that ingests raw wearable sensor data (HRV, sleep stages, heart rate zones, SpO2, skin temperature), processes it through a Bronze → Silver → Gold medallion architecture, and computes a science-based recovery score.

Built with automatic data quality validation that quarantined 53 bad records (5.9%), full data lineage tracking, and a live Streamlit dashboard. Discovered that sleep under 6 hours drops recovery by 11 points, with HRV-Recovery correlation of r = 0.473.

Featured Project

SaaS Funnel Leakage & Revenue Impact Analysis

End-to-end product analytics project that simulates a 20,000-user B2B SaaS funnel to identify where revenue leaks across the Signup → Activation → Trial → Subscription pipeline.

Discovered that activated users convert at 30.3% vs 0.7% for non-activated (43x uplift). Identified $466K+ in recoverable annual revenue through activation optimization, channel-specific fixes, and mobile payment friction reduction.
